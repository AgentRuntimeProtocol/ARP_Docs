Templates are starter repos that help you implement ARP services quickly while staying aligned with the ARP Standard v1 contracts.

:::note Standard vs. template

Templates are not normative. They are scaffolding.
The ARP Standard (spec files) remains the source of truth.

:::

## Available templates (v0.x)

Component templates (service skeletons):
- `ARP_Template_RunGateway`
- `ARP_Template_RunCoordinator`
- `ARP_Template_AtomicExecutor`
- `ARP_Template_CompositeExecutor`
- `ARP_Template_NodeRegistry`
- `ARP_Template_SelectionService`
- `ARP_Template_PDP`

These are intended to be forked and customized.
